Food Resources in Manhattan

New York Common Pantry

8 E 109th St, New York, NY 10029
Contact: (917) 720-9700
Anyone who wishes to register as a client to receive food can stop by the Help 365 window. Must bring an ID for all members in household.
Hours:
Weekdays: 9am-6pm
Sunday: 2pm-6pm

Food Bank

252 W 116th St, New York, NY 10026
Contact: (212) 566-7855
Nonprofit organization with a network of 1,200 emergency and community food providers. Provides approximately 400,000 free meals a day.
Hours:
Weekdays: 10am-4pm
Closed on the weekends.

St. Francis Food Pantries

450 Fashion Ave # 2306, New York, NY 10123
Contact: (212) 279-6171
A network of food and service programs that provides services to alleviate physical/emotional hunger, clothe, and counsel. This organization focuses on providing and supporting services that eradicate both hunger and the structural causes of hunger including a variety of charitable programs.
Hours:
Weekdays: 9-9:15am
Closed on the weekends.
