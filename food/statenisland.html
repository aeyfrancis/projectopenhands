Food Resources in Staten Island

Project Hospitality

514 Bay St, Staten Island, NY 10304
Contact: (718) 273-6737
Organization that focuses on reaching out to community members in need through advocacy and the establishment of a comprehensive continuum of care that includes the provision of food, clothing, and shelter. Services extend to provide physical/mental health care, substance abuse treatment, education, vocational training, legal assistance, and transitional/permanent housing.
Hours:
Weekday hours: 5-10pm
Closed on the weekend.

Trinity Lutheran Church

309 St. Paul’s Ave., 10304
Contact: (718) 447-0526
With no eligibility restrictions, hot “sit-down” meals are offered to all without regard to age, gender, race, income, or denominational affiliation. Between 12:00 noon and 1:00 P.M. our Soup Kitchen meals consist of a healthy, diverse menu that includes fresh produce and meat/proteins.
Pantry hours: Sat. 11AM-1PM
Soup kitchen: Sat. Noon-1PM.

Port Richmond Community Food Pantry

2134 Richmond Terrace (across from Faber Park) Port Richmond, Staten Island
Contact: 718.808.1840
Free food and services of the pantry are open and available to anyone --there is no need to document individual or household income. You can shop at the pantry once each month and choose from available foods so you can take home what your family will eat.
Hours:
Wednesday from 10 a.m. to 4 p.m.
Friday from 2 p.m. to 8 p.m.
Saturday from 10 a.m. to 2 p.m.
