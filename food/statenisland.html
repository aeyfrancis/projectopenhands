Food Resources in Staten Island

Project Hospitality

514 Bay St, Staten Island, NY 10304
Contact: (718) 273-6737
Organization that focuses on reaching out to community members in need. They believe that through advocacy and an establishment of a comprehensive continuum of care that includes the provision of food, clothing, and shelter, they can efficiently accomplish their goals. Services include providing physical/mental health care, substance abuse treatment, education, vocational training, legal assistance, and transitional/permanent housing.
Hours:
Weekday hours: 5-10pm
Closed on the weekends.

Trinity Lutheran Church

309 St. Paul’s Ave., 10304
Contact: (718) 447-0526
With no eligibility restrictions, hot “sit-down” meals are offered to all without regard to age, gender, race, income, or denominational affiliation. Between 12:00 noon and 1:00 P.M. their soup kitchen meals consist of a healthy, diverse menu that includes fresh produce and meat/proteins.
Pantry hours: Saturday hours: 11am-1pm
Soup kitchen hours: Saturday hours: 12-1pm

Port Richmond Community Food Pantry

2134 Richmond Terrace (across from Faber Park) Port Richmond, Staten Island
Contact: (718) 808-1840
Port Richmond provides free food services to everybody. Documents for individual or household incomes are not required You can shop at the pantry once each month and choose from available foods.

Wednesday hours: 10am-4pm
Friday hours: 2-8pm
Saturday hours: 10am-2pm
