Law Firms in Staten Island

Legal Services NYC

36 Richmond Terrace # 205, Staten Island, NY 10301
Contact: (718)233-6480
Legal Services NYC is comprised of approximately 500 dedicated staff members who provide legal services to low income New Yorkers. Helping more than 80,000 clients annually, this firm fights poverty and seeks racial, social, and economic justice for the less fortunate.

Legal Aid Society

60 Bay St #22, Staten Island, NY 10301
Contact: (718) 273-6677
The Legal Aid Society is a private, non profit legal service organization. Being the oldest and largest legal service in the nation, its mission is to enhance the belief that no New Yorker should be denied access to justice due to poverty. The society handles 300,000 cases a year and provides a range of services in the Civil, Criminal, and Juvenile Rights Practices.

Make the Road New York

161 Port Richmond Ave, Staten Island, NY 10302
Contact:(718) 727-1222
Make the Road is comprised of over 20,000 members that come from over 25 different countries. Membership, drawing from surrounding communities throughout the five boroughs, is established to serve the poor and working class communities.
