Hospitals in Bronx

Tremont Health Center

4215 Third Avenue, 2nd floor, Bronx, NY 10457
Contact:(718) 294-5891
Community health care center that provides primary care, adult medicine, pre/post natal care, pediatrics, HIV/STI testing/treatment, and nutrition services. Must provide photo ID, proof of address, and income documents.
Hours:
Weekdays: 8am-5pm, Tuesdays: 8am-7pm.
Closed on Sundays.

Lincoln Medical Center

234 E 149th St, Bronx, NY 10451
Contact: (718) 579-5000
As one of the premier trauma centers in the country, this hospital provides comprehensive primary, secondary, and tertiary care that includes a variety of medical specialties and diagnostic services. Highly trained clinicians provide patient-centered services.

24 hrs

Bronx-Lebanon Hospital Center

1650 Grand Concourse, Bronx, NY 10457
Contact: (718) 590-1800
The Bronx-Lebanon Hospital Center is the largest nonprofit health care system that provides more than 1.1 million annual visits. This hospital center reflects its commitment to provide charity care and financial assistance through its Charity Care Policy. It also recognizes and supports those who are uninsured/underinsured and require medical assistance.
Hours:
24 hrs
